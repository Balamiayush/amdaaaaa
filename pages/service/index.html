About AMDA Nepal

The Association of Medical Doctors of Asia-Nepal (AMDA Nepal) is dedicated to providing quality healthcare services and medical assistance throughout Nepal.

AMDA Nepal was established with the mission to improve healthcare access and quality across Nepal, especially in underserved and remote areas. Our network of dedicated medical professionals works tirelessly to bring essential healthcare services to communities in need.

Through our various centers of excellence and specialized programs, we aim to address key healthcare challenges facing Nepal today. From disaster response to preventive care initiatives, AMDA Nepal is at the forefront of healthcare innovation and service delivery.
