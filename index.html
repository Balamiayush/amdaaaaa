Welcome to AMDA Nepal

Association of Medical Doctors of Asia-Nepal (AMDA-Nepal) was established in 1989 as a chapter of AMDA-International, Japan and was officially registered in the government office as an NGO on March 4, 1991. It is a humanitarian, nonprofit-making, non-political, non-sectarian, Non-Governmental Organization working with its mission to promote the health and well-being of the underprivileged and marginalized people under the slogan:
“Better quality of life for better future”.

What We Do

AMDA-Nepal provides various healthcare services and programs to improve the quality of life for underprivileged communities.

Providing quality healthcare services through our network of hospitals and clinics across Nepal.
